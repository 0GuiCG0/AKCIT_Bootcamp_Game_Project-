=== STYLE BIBLE ===
Pixel art, 16-bit era, enemy sprites inspired by classic DOOM (1993) demons.
Strict palette of 6 colors per sprite:
#ffffff (white)
#ffdb03 (yellow)
#ff120a (bright red)
#a50000 (dark red)
#5d0000 (deep crimson)
#332425 (near-black brown)
Top-down view, Bold and aggressive silhouettes, chunky pixel details, no anti-aliasing,
no dithering. Centered subject on uniform white background.
High contrast, gritty and brutal aesthetic. No text, no UI, no borders.
